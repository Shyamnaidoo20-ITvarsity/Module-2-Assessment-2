FOREVER NEW

Forever New is a fashion clothing and accessories brand founded in Melbourne, Australia. One of the fastest growing Australian brands, Forever New emerged in late 2006 as a start up retailer and now trades over 250 stores in ten countries globally.

Newly Launched in South Africa, the Forever New site will become your first choice when looking for the most up to date and current international fashion.

Ever New is the trading name of the brand in selected international locations. As a related company of Forever New Clothing Pty Ltd, selected product available on Forever New Outlet may feature Ever New branded labels. Our signature style, quality and overall fit remains consistent across both brand names.

Our brand celebrates the feminine beauty of women, a signature style flowing through every creative element.

Collections are inspired by global trends in art, film, music and theatre as well as haute couture runway shows in Paris, London, Milan and New York.
