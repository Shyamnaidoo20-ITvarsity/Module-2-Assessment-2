CALISTOS

The hearty family brand that has grown tremendously since it’s humble beginnings 24 years ago and is a name that has become synonymous with Portuguese food lovers. Since then the brand has gained popularity for its diverse menu offering, from the best flamed grilled Portuguese chicken to the finest seafood and meat, with meals that are freshly prepared in the traditional Portuguese way using only the freshest, finest ingredients available.

Its authentic character, vibrant and electric atmosphere fills these eateries with a delightful experience that will bring you back time and time again. Today, the brand is a leader in Portuguese cuisine and boasts 11 restaurants across the country. It’s more than a restaurant, it’s an experience and the best experience south of Portugal.
